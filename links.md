


## AI Model & Research Websites

- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) — A central hub for discovering, sharing, and using pre-trained AI models and datasets.

- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/) — A data science platform with datasets, models, and competitions for hands-on machine learning practice.

- [Papers with Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) — Links AI research papers to their code implementations and benchmarks so you can see how models perform.

- [Open LLM Leaderboard](https://huggingface.co/spaces/open-llm-leaderboard/open_llm_leaderboard) — Ranks large language models based on standardized evaluation benchmarks.

- [Chatbot Arena](https://arena.ai/) — Lets users compare AI models side-by-side through live conversations and voting.

- [HELM (Stanford)](https://crfm.stanford.edu/helm/) — Provides a comprehensive evaluation framework for analyzing LLM performance, fairness, and reliability.

- [Replicate](https://replicate.com/) — Allows you to run and experiment with AI models directly in the browser via simple APIs.

- [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/) — Aggregates multiple LLM APIs so you can compare models, pricing, and performance in one place.

- [Together AI](https://www.together.ai/) — Offers access to open-source models and infrastructure for building and deploying AI applications.

- [Artificial Analysis](https://artificialanalysis.ai/) — Tracks and compares AI models based on performance, speed, and cost metrics.

- [Epoch AI](https://epochai.org/) — Research organization analyzing long-term trends in AI development, scaling, and compute.
