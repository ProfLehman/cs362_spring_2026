# CS 362 – Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning  
## Ethics Paper – 100 Points  
**Due:** Tuesday, April 2  

---

## Project Description

Write a comprehensive ethics paper that explores **one specific ethical issue** arising from the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).  

Your paper should:

- Highlight the complexities of the issue  
- Integrate **biblical principles**
- Include **personal faith reflections**
- Propose thoughtful considerations or solutions  

Focus your paper on **one primary area** rather than providing a shallow summary of multiple issues.

---

## Length and Format

- Minimum **2,500 words**
- Choose either **APA** or **MLA** format  
  - The selected format must be used **consistently throughout**
- Required sections:
  - Title Page  
  - Abstract  
  - Main Body  
  - References Page  
- Minimum of **five sources**
  - Academic journals  
  - Books  
  - Reputable news outlets  
  - Official websites  
  - At least **one peer-reviewed journal article**

---

## Content Requirements

### 1. Introduction (10 points)

- Briefly introduce the ethical issue related to AI/ML.
- Provide context explaining why the issue is significant.
- Possible topics include:
  - Privacy
  - Bias and fairness
  - Accountability
  - Transparency
  - Misuse of technology
- Length: **One or two paragraphs**

---

### 2. AI & Machine Learning Background (10 points)

- Provide a brief background of the AI/ML technology connected to your issue.
- Explain how the technology has evolved.
- Example:
  - If discussing neural networks, provide a one- to two-paragraph explanation of neural networks.
- Length: **One or two paragraphs**

---

### 3. Body – Issue Analysis (25 points)

- Provide specific examples or case studies.
- Use:
  - Recent news
  - Academic research
  - Ethical watchdog reports
- Discuss:
  - Negative instances
  - Positive efforts to mitigate concerns
- Address broader implications:
  - Social divides
  - Erosion of norms
  - Impacts on democracy
  - Human rights
- Length: **Four or more paragraphs**

---

### 4. Biblical Integration (10 points)

- Discuss how biblical principles inform understanding of the issue.
- Reference:
  - Specific scriptures
  - Biblical themes related to ethics, technology, and stewardship
- If you are not a Christian, you are still expected to thoughtfully engage this section.
- Length: **Two or three paragraphs**

---

### 5. Personal Faith Reflection (10 points)

- Reflect on how your personal faith influences your perspective.
- Discuss:
  - Personal beliefs
  - Values
  - Experiences
- Length: **Two or three paragraphs**

---

### 6. Conclusion and Solutions (10 points)

- Summarize your findings.
- Propose thoughtful solutions or considerations.
- Solutions should be guided by:
  - Biblical principles
  - Personal faith reflection
- Length: **Two or three paragraphs**

---

## Submission Requirements

- Submit a **Grammarly report PDF**
  - Minimum performance score of **90**
- Submit paper through **Turnitin**
  - Must meet academic integrity standards
- Students must submit electronic or paper copies of all sources used, with cited portions clearly highlighted. This requirement ensures that sources have been thoroughly read and appropriately integrated into the paper.
